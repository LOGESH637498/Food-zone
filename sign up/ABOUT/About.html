ABOUT US

Being among the first few entrants, Foodzone has successfully pioneered the hyperlocal commerce industry in India, launching Food Delivery in 2014 and Quick Commerce in 2020. Due to the pioneering status of Foodzone, it is well-recognised as a leader in innovation in hyperlocal commerce and as a brand synonymous with the categories it is present in.
